Introducing Mantri Lithos Apartments
Mantri Lithos is a luxurious project coming up inside MANYATA TECHPARK. Mantri Lithos consist of 2 and 3 BHK spacious apartments. This Apartment project is coming up in total land area of 6 acres consisting of 8 towers and 552 luxury apartments.

Mantri Lithos Location Unique selling proposition:
  • Located adjacent to Manyata Tech Park.
  • Outer Ring Road joins BIAL towards the north and joins Bangalore city towards the south
  • Well connected to city’s business districts, educational institutions and shopping areas.
  • Schools of national and international repute are in close vicinity.
  • Columbia Asia Hospital within 3 kms.
  • Mere 8 kms from M G Road and CBD areas.


Mantri Lithos High Points
  • New Iconic Structure
  • Distinctive Angular form of the building compliments the name Lithos, meaning Rocky edges.
  • Naturally ventilated & well lit Designer Apartments with Contemporary looks, designed by International Architects.
  • Each core plate has four units with two units overlooking the landscaped internal courtyard and other two units gets the vanishing view of neighbouring urban green.
  • Pre-certified to be “Green Building“ certified.
  • One of its kind of high end clubhouse which is literally submerged with green/landscaped roof.
  • Undulating roofscape with terrace
